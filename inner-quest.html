Your Inner Quest

A 10‑Day Pilgrimage of Thought

There is a pilgrimage that begins not with a step, but with a thought. It moves through subtle regions of awareness, gathering light. As Sister Shivani travels across the bioregions, you travel inward — a 10‑day quest toward a place of original freedom.

Each stop is a lens. As the outer journey converges, let your thoughts lovingly reach the soil, water, and souls of each region. Attention becomes offering; remembrance becomes gentle power. A pattern reveals itself — peace was never lost, only quiet.

By the time Cascadia receives the closing breath, an inner alignment forms: a recognition of timeless peace and benevolent strength within. From this shared field we can accomplish pure things together.
